🕯️ What is a candlestick?

Each candle summarises one period (a day, a week, etc.) of trading. Four numbers — Open, High, Low, Close — drawn as a body and two wicks.

🟢 Bullish (green) candle

Close is above the open — buyers won the period. The body shows the open→close range; wicks show the extremes traded.

🔴 Bearish (red) candle

Close is below the open — sellers won the period. Same anatomy, just inverted body direction.
